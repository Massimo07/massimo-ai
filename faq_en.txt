Q: How do I register for Live On Plus?
Registration is free. You need name, surname, fiscal code, email, and phone.

Q: What are the membership types?
You can register as a Customer and get up to 60% discount on products.

Q: What is Shop 1?
Shop 1 is the main shop: you can buy all products with the maximum discount for your level.

Q: What is Shop 2?
Shop 2 is a special shop. To access it, you must have at least 10 points on Shop 1. You can buy on Shop 2 up to 20% more points than you made on Shop 1.
